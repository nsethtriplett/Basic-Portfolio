About me

I was born in Columbia, Missouri on July 30th, 1996. This is not where or when I chose to be born, but we don't always get to choose our circumstances. I grew up in a loving home with three other siblings-- two brothers and one sister (she also happens to be my favorite sister). Their names are Noah (25), Hannah (18), and Levi (14).

In 2005 we moved to Joplin, Missouri, and I've lived there for roughly 12 years. I attended high school and was then shipped off to college, eventually transferring to two other colleges until I realized that learning code could be a fresh start. I wanted financial freedom, physcial freedom to go where I please, and an opportunity to see what could happen if I chose a path that differed from what had been planned for me for most of my life.
