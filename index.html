About me

I grew up in a loving home with three other siblings-- two brothers and a sister that shouldv'e been my third brother. Their names are Noah (who is 25), Hannah (18), and Levi (14).

In 2005 we moved to Joplin, Missouri, and I've lived there for roughly 12 years (or I've roughly lived there for 12 years). I attended high school and was then shipped off to college, eventually transferring to two other colleges until I realize I wanted a different way of life entirely. I want financial freedom, physcial freedom to go where I please, and any other kind of freedom that the world has to offer ;P.
